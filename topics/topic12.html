Computer Networks

A computer network is a group of interconnected devices that communicate and share resources. The internet itself is a massive global network. Networks enable communication, data transfer, remote access, and resource sharing.

Types of Networks

- **LAN (Local Area Network)** – small area like home, office, school.
- **WAN (Wide Area Network)** – connects large geographical areas.
- **MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)** – used in cities.
- **PAN (Personal Area Network)** – Bluetooth devices.

Network Devices

Routers, switches, modems, repeaters, and access points help route data and maintain network stability. Each device has a specific function in ensuring data flows efficiently.

Protocols & IP Addressing

Protocols like TCP/IP, HTTP, HTTPS, and FTP define rules for communication. Every device on a network has an IP address that helps identify it. IPv4 and IPv6 are the two versions of addressing systems.
