Cybersecurity Basics

Cybersecurity protects computers, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. As technology grows, cyber threats such as malware, ransomware, phishing, and data breaches have become more common.

Common Cyber Threats

- **Malware** – harmful software that damages systems.
- **Phishing** – fake messages used to steal personal information.
- **Ransomware** – locks your data until a ransom is paid.
- **DDoS Attacks** – overloads servers to shut them down.

Protection Techniques

Cybersecurity uses firewalls, antivirus systems, encryption, strong passwords, and authentication methods like OTP and biometrics. Regular updates and backups are essential for protection.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Every online system needs cybersecurity. From bank accounts to social media, user data must be protected. Cyber professionals are in huge demand globally.
