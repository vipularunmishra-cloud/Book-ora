Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence is the ability of computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, decision-making, and understanding language or images. AI powers virtual assistants, self-driving cars, search engines, and more.

Types of AI

- **Narrow AI** – specialized tasks like Siri, Google Maps.
- **General AI** – human-like intelligence (still theoretical).
- **Machine Learning** – computers learn patterns from data.
- **Deep Learning** – advanced form using neural networks.

Applications of AI

AI is used in healthcare, finance, gaming, robotics, education, marketing, and cybersecurity. It analyzes huge amounts of data and makes predictions faster than humans.

Why AI is the Future

AI is revolutionizing industries, creating new jobs, and automating repetitive tasks. Learning AI concepts opens opportunities in data science, automation, and software engineering.
