Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity protects computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. It is one of the fastest-growing fields.

Types of Cyber Attacks

Phishing: Fake emails to steal information. Malware: Viruses, trojans, ransomware. DDoS: Overloading servers. SQL Injection: Injecting malicious queries. Man-in-the-Middle: Intercepting communication.

Security Practices

- Strong passwords - Two-factor authentication - Encryption - Firewalls - Regular updates - Backups

Cybersecurity Careers

Ethical hacker, penetration tester, SOC analyst, security engineer. This field is in extremely high demand worldwide.
