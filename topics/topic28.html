Computer Networking

Computer networking refers to connecting computers and devices so they can share data and resources efficiently.

Network Types

  • LAN: Local network inside a building.
  • WAN: Larger networks across cities/countries.
  • MAN: Metropolitan area networks.

Devices

  • Router
  • Switch
  • Modem
  • Access Point

Protocols

Most common networking protocol is TCP/IP which runs the whole internet.
