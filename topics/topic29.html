Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT refers to billions of devices connected to the internet that collect and share data, enabling automation and smart functionality.

Examples

  • Smart homes (lights, AC, Alexa)
  • Smartwatches and health tracking
  • IoT cars and navigation
  • Surveillance systems

How IoT Works

Sensors → Data → Cloud → Automation/Action.

Challenges

Security risks, privacy concerns, large data management.
