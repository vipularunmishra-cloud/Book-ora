Computer Networks

1. Introduction

Computer Networks allow devices to communicate and share resources. The internet itself is the largest network ever created.

2. Types of Networks

  • LAN (Local Area Network)
  • MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)
  • WAN (Wide Area Network)
  • PAN (Personal Area Network)

3. Important Concepts

  • IP Address
  • Router
  • Switch
  • Packets
  • Protocols (TCP, UDP, HTTP, FTP)

4. OSI Model

The OSI Model has 7 layers:

  1. Physical
  2. Data Link
  3. Network
  4. Transport
  5. Session
  6. Presentation
  7. Application

5. Summary

Networks form the backbone of the internet and communication. Understanding them is essential for cybersecurity and IT fields.
