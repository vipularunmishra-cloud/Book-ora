Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks aim to access, change, or destroy information, steal money, or disrupt services.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

As everything becomes digital—banking, healthcare, education, communication—cyber threats have increased. Hackers constantly try to break into systems for profit or disruption.

Common Cyber Threats

1. Malware

Software designed to harm systems—viruses, worms, Trojans, ransomware.

2. Phishing

Fake emails or messages trick users into revealing personal information.

3. DDoS Attacks

Overloading a server to make a service unavailable.

4. SQL Injection

Injecting malicious SQL queries into websites to steal data.

5. Man-in-the-Middle Attack

Intercepting communication between two people.

Best Security Practices

  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never click unknown links
  • Keep software updated
  • Avoid using public WiFi for banking

Cybersecurity is essential to survive in a connected world.
